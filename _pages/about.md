## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the **Max Planck Institute Security & Privacy Group**. I graduated from **EPFL** in June 2023 with a Ph.D. in Computer Science. My research focuses on **applied cryptography and security**, with particular interests in:

- **Zero-Knowledge Proof Systems**
- **Secure Multi-Party Computation**
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography**

## Education

* **Ph.D. in Computer Science**, EPFL, 2018 - 2023
* **B.Sc.**, Moscow State University, 2014 - 2018
